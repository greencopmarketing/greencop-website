Why Green COP

Driven by sustainability, built for partnership.

Green COP was founded by a team of professionals dedicated to driving environmental sustainability. Our ethos centres on fulfilling our customers' unique needs to help them achieve their business goals, allowing us to cultivate partnerships built on mutual respect and trust.

We aim to add value to almost all types of plant-based waste, building towards a shared goal: a true circular economy for the maritime and transportation sectors.
